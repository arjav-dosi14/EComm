shopping_bag Orders

How do I place an order? expand_more
To place an order, simply browse our shop, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your shipping and payment information.
Can I cancel an order? expand_more
Orders can be canceled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the processing begins. Please contact support immediately if you need to make changes.

local_shipping Shipping

How long does delivery take? expand_more
Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. International shipping can take 7-14 business days depending on your location.
Do you offer express shipping? expand_more
Yes, we offer next-day and 2-day express shipping for most regions. Options and costs are displayed at checkout.

payments Payments

What payment methods do you accept? expand_more
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
Is online payment secure? expand_more
Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and secure payment gateways to ensure your financial data is always protected.

assignment_return Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy? expand_more
We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. Some exclusions apply for hygiene products.
How do refunds work? expand_more
Once we receive your return, we will inspect it and process your refund within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.
